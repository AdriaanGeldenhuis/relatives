────────────────────────
APP OVERVIEW
────────────────────────
Relatives is an all-in-one family management app designed to keep families connected,
organized, and safe in daily life.

It combines:
- Live family location tracking
- Shared shopping lists and to-do lists
- Shared notes
- Shared family calendar and reminders
- Study programs and routines
- Weather information
- AI Voice commands and Full AI Q & A.
- An AI assistant for guidance and questions

Relatives is mobile-first and designed primarily for phones.

────────────────────────
ACCOUNT, FAMILY & USERS
────────────────────────
• Each person has their own account.
• One person creates a family and becomes the Owner.
• Family members are invited by the Owner or Admin.
• Only people in the same family can see each other’s data.

Roles:
- Owner: manages family, members, and billing.
- Admin: manages members and content.
- Member: normal usage only.

Common questions:
- “How do I invite someone?” → Family/Admin section → Invite member.
- “Why can’t I see someone?” → They must accept the invite and be logged in.
- “Can I remove someone?” → Yes, Owner or Admin only.

PRICING, TRIAL & SUBSCRIPTIONS
────────────────────────
Relatives uses a free trial followed by a paid subscription.

Trial:
- Every new user gets a 3-day free trial.
- During the trial, all features are available.
- After the trial ends, the app switches to view-only mode until payment.

Subscriptions (monthly):
- R109: up to 3 family members
- R149: up to 6 family members
- R199: more than 6 family members

Yearly plans may be available at a discounted rate.

Important:
- Billing is managed by the Owner.
- If payment fails, there may be a short grace period.
- Family size limits are enforced automatically.

Common questions:
- “Why can’t I add more people?” → Your plan limit has been reached.
- “Why is the app locked?” → Trial ended or payment issue.
- “Can I downgrade or upgrade?” → Yes, via the subscription screen.

────────────────────────
LOCATION TRACKING
────────────────────────
Relatives supports real-time family location tracking.

Key points:
- Tracking works in the background via the native app.
- Only family members can see locations.
- Each person controls whether they share their location.

What users see:
- Live map with family members
- Last known location
- Last updated time

Common problems & fixes:
- Tracking not updating:
  1. Location permission must be set to “Allow all the time”.
  2. Battery optimization must be disabled for Relatives.
  3. Internet connection must be active.
- Can’t see someone:
  - They must be logged in and have tracking enabled.

Privacy:
- No public sharing.
- Location data is private to the family only.
